Security & Privacy

Options marked with this symbol are critical edits, and will disconnect your account from all sessions.

E-mail Address

This is the e-mail address connected to this account and used to log in.

Password

This is the password you use to log-in into your shoshin.moe account.

Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) is a secure method to verify that the person trying to log-in into the account
is actually the owner of it, by sending a mail to the E-mail Address associated with the account.

It's highly suggested to keep this enabled, as if someone tries to access your account. they will need to have access to your e-mail inbox as well in order to succeed in.

Profile Visibility

Set your profile visibility to either public or private.
Public profiles are visible to everyone, while private profiles are only visible to you and your friends.

Delete Account

This is a critical action and will delete your account permanently from our servers, along with
all the uploads you've made with it. This is irreversible and cannot be undone even by the administrators.
