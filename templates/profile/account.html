Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) is a secure method to verify that the person trying to log-in into the account
is actually the owner of it, by sending a mail to the E-mail Address associated with the account.

It's highly suggested to keep this enabled, as if someone tries to access your account. they will need to have access to your e-mail inbox as well in order to succeed in.
