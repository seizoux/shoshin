Community Guidelines for Shoshin.moe

Last Updated: [06/30/2024]

1. Introduction

Welcome to Shoshin.moe! Our community guidelines aim to ensure a safe, respectful, and positive environment for everyone. By participating in our community, you agree to adhere to these guidelines. Violations of these guidelines may result in the suspension or termination of your account without notice.

2. Respect and Civility

Treat everyone with respect. Harassment, hate speech, bullying, and any form of discrimination will not be tolerated. This includes but is not limited to:

  • Racial, ethnic, or cultural insults.
  • Sexist comments or behavior.
  • Homophobic or transphobic remarks.
  • Religious intolerance.
  • Personal attacks or threats.

3. Posting Content

When publishing media, leaving comments, or updating your profile, ensure that your content is appropriate and adheres to our guidelines. Do not post:

  • Pornographic or sexually explicit material.
  • Violent or graphic content.
  • Hateful or discriminatory content.
  • Spam, misleading information, or clickbait.
  • Personal information of others without their consent.
  • Content that infringes on intellectual property rights.
  • Illegal activities or the promotion of illegal activities.

4. Profile Customization

You may customize your profile with a unique profile picture, banner image, biography, and nickname. Ensure that your profile content adheres to our guidelines and does not include any prohibited material. Specifically:

  • Profile pictures and banner images must not contain nudity, sexually explicit content, or graphic violence.
  • Biographies and nicknames must not contain offensive language, hate speech, or personal attacks.
  • Any impersonation of other users, public figures, or entities is strictly prohibited.

5. Interactions with Others

You can follow other users, like their posts, and post content visible only to close friends. When interacting with others:

  • Be respectful and considerate in your comments and messages. Personal attacks, harassment, and abuse will not be tolerated.
  • Do not engage in spamming, excessive tagging, or unwanted solicitation.
  • Respect others' privacy and do not share their private information without consent.
  • Report any abusive or inappropriate behavior to our support team immediately.
  • Do not create or share content that encourages self-harm or suicide.

6. Privacy and Security

Protect your personal information and be cautious about sharing sensitive details. We recommend using strong passwords and enabling two-factor authentication if available. If you notice any suspicious activity, report it to us immediately. Specifically:

  • Do not share your login credentials with anyone.
  • Report phishing attempts or any suspicious messages to our support team.
  • Do not use the Website for any form of illegal activities, including hacking, fraud, or distribution of malware.

7. Reporting Violations

If you encounter any content or behavior that violates these guidelines, please report it to our support team at helpdesk@shoshin.moe. We take all reports seriously and will investigate and take appropriate action. This may include content removal, account suspension, or termination.

8. Consequences of Violations

Violating these guidelines may result in the removal of content, suspension, or termination of your account. We reserve the right to take any action we deem necessary to maintain a safe and respectful community. Repeated violations or severe breaches of these guidelines will result in permanent bans. Specific actions that will result in consequences include:

  • Posting illegal content or engaging in illegal activities.
  • Harassing, threatening, or bullying other users.
  • Using multiple accounts to evade suspensions or bans.
  • Engaging in spamming or the distribution of unsolicited messages.
  • Impersonating other users, public figures, or entities.

9. Intellectual Property

Respect the intellectual property rights of others. Do not share or distribute content that you do not own or have permission to use. If you believe your intellectual property rights have been infringed, please report it to our support team with the necessary details to investigate and take action.

11. Changes to the Guidelines

We may update these guidelines from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new guidelines on this page. You are advised to review these guidelines periodically for any updates. Continued use of the Website following the posting of changes constitutes your acceptance of such changes.
